FAQ

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Use the HZ printer service; connect to Eduroam and follow the printing instructions on Learn (MyHZ).

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Use the HZ scanning stations and have them email you the PDF or save to your HZ files.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Sign in with your HZ account, select items and checkout using the provided payment methods.

How can you book a project space?

Use the room booking system on Learn or campus intranet.

What are the instructions for parking at HZ?

Check the HZ parking page for rates and rules; visitors may need a permit.
